Where London Cycles

This interactive visualization explores cycling patterns across London using Transport for London's Santander Cycles data.

The Data

Based on the most popular A to B trips and return-to-origin routes with Santander cycle rentals over a 12-month period in London. The top 570 routes are visualized showing where Londoners cycle most frequently.

How to Use

Top A to B Trips: Explore the 20 most popular point-to-point journeys. Green marker (A) shows origin, red marker (B) shows destination.

Top Return-to-Origin: See the 20 most popular stations where people rent and return bikes to the same location.

🐝 Hive Network: View all 570 routes visualized as a network. Routes shown as arcs represent connection volume, not actual cycling paths.
