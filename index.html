Where London Cycles

The Data

Based on each Santander Cycle dock's most popular destination over a 12-month period (2024/25) in London. The top 570 routes are visualized showing where Londoners cycle most frequently.

How to Use

One-way Trips: Explore the 20 most popular point-to-point journeys per cycle dock. Green marker (A) shows origin, red marker (B) shows destination.

Return-to-Origin Trips: See the 20 most popular unique stations where people rent and return bikes to the same location.

🐝 Hive Network: View all 570 routes visualized as a network. Routes shown as arcs with colour coded connectors represent connection volume, not actual cycling paths.
